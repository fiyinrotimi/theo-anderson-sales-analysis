# Theo-Anderson Stores Sales Dashboard (2024)

## 🎯 The Business Challenge
Management needed visibility into profitability metrics across different days of the week to optimize store operations and marketing spend.

![Dashboard Screenshot](11995.jpg)

## 💡 Key Insights Discovered
* **Profit Margins:** Maintained a healthy 44% profit margin on over $620k in total sales.
* **Weekend Inefficiencies:** Identified that 67% of profits occur on weekdays. Specifically pinpointed the "Bottom 10" locations for weekend profits (e.g., Pomona), providing a roadmap for targeted promotions.
* **Customer Volume:** Analyzed purchasing behavior across nearly 350,000 customers.

* **Data Visualization:** Microsoft Power BI & Advanced Excel
* **Data Transformation:** Data cleaning and modeling
* **Business Intelligence:** KPI tracking and data storytelling
